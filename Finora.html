Finora

Finora Mobile App

Overview

Finora is a cutting-edge digital banking platform designed to provide tech-savvy, urban individuals with a modern, convenient, and secure way to manage their finances. The app focuses on simplifying transactions, supporting digital wallets like Apple Pay and Samsung Pay, and offering intuitive budgeting tools for young professionals and students

Problem Statement

As financial services become more digital, users in urban areas demand seamless, fast, and secure digital banking experiences. Traditional banking apps often lack integration with modern payment systems and do not provide personalized tools to manage finances effectively. VirtualBank was created to address these pain points.

Design Process

For the Finora project, the design process began with discovery, where I conducted extensive research into digital banking trends and user needs, including interviews with potential users to identify pain points such as difficulty navigating complex interfaces and lack of responsive design. During the define phase, I crafted a problem statement and developed user personas, focusing on tech-savvy individuals and small business owners, while mapping out user journeys to pinpoint areas for improvement. In the ideation phase, I proposed solutions like seamless navigation, intuitive dashboards, and adaptive designs to ensure usability across devices, prioritizing features with a MoSCoW matrix. Moving into design, I created low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes using Figma, emphasizing a professional yet approachable aesthetic with clean layouts and accessible typography. These designs were validated through usability testing, where participants provided feedback on navigation and feature clarity, leading to iterative improvements, such as refining menu structures and enhancing visual hierarchy. During the development handoff, I provided detailed design specifications, collaborated with developers during sprints, and resolved queries to maintain design integrity. Post-launch, I supported user onboarding with walkthroughs and tooltips, collected user feedback, and implemented iterative updates like customizable dashboards and enhanced data visualization features to elevate the user experience further.
