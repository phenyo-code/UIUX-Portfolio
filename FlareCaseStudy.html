FLARE

E-commerce Mobile App

Overview

FLARE is an e-commerce app dedicated to selling clothing, with a focus on showcasing and supporting local South African fashion brands. The goal of the project was to create a seamless shopping experience that promotes local designers and offers a wide variety of fashion choices, from everyday wear to high-end fashion.

Role

UI/UX Design

Tools

Figma
Sketch

Timeline

June 2023 - July 2023

Problem Statement

The challenge was to create an intuitive user experience that not only made it easy for users to browse and purchase clothing but also highlighted the local fashion brands and their unique products.
